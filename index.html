ÉLAN Brow Gel Tint

What is ÉLAN Gel Tint designed for?

It provides intense and even brow tinting, has a gel texture for easy application and additionally cares for the skin and hairs thanks to the vegan protein in the composition.

What should I mix ÉLAN Gel Tint with?

The paint must be mixed with Oxidant 1% or Oxidizing Emulsion 3.5% from ÉLAN.

What is the difference between oxidants?

  • Oxidant 1% — gives a more natural shade, does not darken the hairs.
  • Oxidizing Emulsion 3.5% — creates a richer shade on both skin and hairs.

How to use Gel Tint correctly?

  1. Cleanse the eyebrows before tinting (ÉLAN DETOX-MOUSSE is recommended).
  2. Build the desired eyebrow shape.
  3. Mix the tint with an oxidant in a 1:1 ratio.
  4. Apply the mixture, leave for 5-15 minutes depending on the desired result.
  5. Rinse off with a damp cotton pad or ÉLAN DETOX-MOUSSE.
  6. Fix the result with the ÉLAN COLOUR FIX fluid.

Does the colour of the tint change after opening?

Yes, the colour may vary from light brown to black due to contact with air. This is normal and does not affect the tinting result.

Can I use Gel Tint for lashes?

No, the product is intended for eyebrows only.

What should I do if the tint gets outside the eyebrow shape?

Immediately remove with a cotton swab moistened with water, not allowing the paint to dry on the skin.

How long does the tinting result last?

The duration of the effect depends on the skin type, care and individual characteristics, usually up to several weeks

Lash tints

KeraVEG lash lifting system

What is KeraVEG and what is it used for?

KeraVEG is an innovative lash lifting system that curls, strengthens and nurtures lashes with vegan components. It consists of four main products that work in stages to create a defined curl, secure the shape and provide intensive care.

What products are included in the KeraVEG lash lifting system?

The system includes:
  • KeraSoft Lash Lifting Serum (Step 1) — to soften hairs and prepare for reshaping.
  • KeraBoost Lash and Brow Fixing Serum (Step 2) — to fix the curl and shape.
  • KeraTint Lash Tint — for intensive tinting during the second stage of the procedure.
  • KeraCare Lash and Brow Caring Serum (Step 3) — to moisturise, nourish and restore lashes after the procedure.

How does the KeraVEG lash lifting procedure work?

The procedure consists of three main stages:
  • Shaping the lash curl — use KeraSoft Serum Lash Lift to soften and prepare the hairs.
  • Fixing the shape of the lashes — use KeraBoost Serum Fixer for lashes and eyebrows. At the same stage, you can combine fixation with tinting by mixing KeraBoost with KeraTint Lash Tint.
  • Lash care — apply KeraCare Serum Lash and Brow Care to deeply moisturise, strengthen and protect lashes.

How to combine lash lifting and dyeing during the procedure?

At the second stage of the procedure, you can combine lash fixation with tinting. To do this, you need:
  • Mix KeraBoost Lash and Brow Fixing Serum (Step 2) and KeraTint Lash Tint in a 2:1 ratio in a non-metallic container.
  • Apply the mixture to the eyelashes and leave for 7 minutes.
  • After the exposure time has expired, carefully remove the remaining tint and fixative with a brush and then with a microbrush or a cotton swab moistened with water or a cleansing mousse.
  • This allows you to get a uniform colour and reduce the time of the procedure.

What is the exposure time for each product?

KeraSoft Lash Lifting Serum (Step 1):
  • For thick, even lashes — 8-10 minutes.
  • For medium and thin lashes — 5-7 minutes.
  • The maximum time is 10 minutes.
  • KeraBoost Lash and Brow Fixing Serum (Step 2) — 7 minutes (regardless of the condition of the lashes).
  • KeraTint Lash Tint + KeraBoost (Step 2) — 7 minutes.

Does the room temperature affect the procedure?

Yes, temperature affects the reaction rate of the products. In warm rooms, the active ingredients act faster, so you should take this into account when determining the exposure time.

Do I need to rinse off the products after each step?

So, after each stage, the remaining products must be removed:
  • After KeraSoft (Step 1), first with a dry brush and then with a microbrush or cotton swab dampened with water.
  • After KeraBoost (Step 2) or KeraBoost + KeraTint mix, use a dry brush and then a microbrush or cleansing mousse.
  • KeraCare (Step 3) does not need to be rinsed off — it should be left until it is completely absorbed.

How to care for lashes after the procedure?

After the lash lifting procedure, you should follow these recommendations:
  • Do not visit baths, saunas, solariums, rub your eyes or sleep with your face in a pillow for 24 hours.
  • Avoid contact with aggressive (alcohol lotions, peels, scrubs) for 48 hours.
  • Use lash care products, such as KeraCare Lash and Brow Caring Serum (Step 3).

Eyebrow and Lash Lamination (Flexing) Expert System «SUPERSONIC 2.0»

What is «SUPERSONIC 2.0»?

This is a professional eyebrow and lash shaping system that not only creates the perfect curl, but also cares for the hairs, making them stronger, more elastic and shiny.

What products are included in the lash flexing system?

  • Serum 1 CLEAR is the first stage of the eyebrow lamination and lash lifting procedure, which opens the cuticle of the lashes and remodels their shape.
  • Serum 1 PINK is the first stage of the lash lifting procedure, which opens the lash cuticle and remodels the lash shape.
  • Serum 2 fixes the new shape of the lashes and restores the natural pH.
  • Multivitor is a nourishing product that strengthens hairs after the procedure and completes the chemical process.

Why is Serum 1 PINK pink?

This helps the technician easily track the application area and distribute the product evenly over the lashes.

Is it possible to use Serum 1 pink for eyebrows?

No, this product is designed exclusively for elashes, as it can stain eyebrows pink.

How does Serum 1 work?

  • Opens the lash scales, preparing them for curling.
  • Interacts with disulfide bonds, changing the shape of hairs.
  • Contains keratin and collagen, which protect hairs from damage.
  • Makes lashes elastic and thicker.

How does Serum 2 work?

  • Fixes a new lash shape.
  • Restores the natural pH of the hairs.
  • Gives lashes flexibility, elasticity and shine.

How does Multivitor work?

  • Restores the structure of lashes after flexing.
  • Deeply moisturises and strengthens hair.
  • It fixes the result and adds shine and elasticity.

How to carry out the lash flexing procedure correctly?

  • Cleansing of lashes — use ÉLAN DETOX-MOUSSE.
  • Fixing the silicone pad on the eyelid.
  • Curl formation — application of Serum 1 (3-10 minutes depending on the thickness of the lashes).
  • Fixing the direction of the lashes — applying Serum 2 (the same time as Serum 1).
  • Nourishing lashes — applying Multivitor (5 minutes).
  • At the end of the chemical process, rinse off any residual product with water or ÉLAN DETOX-MOUSSE.

How to determine the exposure time of the products?

  • 6-10 minutes — for thick or straight lashes.
  • 3-6 minutes — for medium or thin eyelashes.
  • Multivector — 5 minutes.

Do I need to dye my lashes after flexing?

Yes, it is recommended to dye the lashes after the procedure for maximum effect.

What recommendations should be followed after the procedure?

For 24 hours, do not wet your lashes, do not sleep with your face in a pillow, avoid saunas and solariums. Do not use aggressive cosmetics (alcohol-containing tonics, scrubs, peels) for 48 hours. Do not comb your lashes or apply mascara for the first 24 hours.

Are there any restrictions for lash flexing?

  • Individual sensitivity to components.
  • Allergic reactions to chemical hairs treatments.
  • Damage to the skin around the eyes, rashes, irritation.
  • Carrying out laser or photographic procedures less than 24 hours before flexing.
